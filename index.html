VibeCheck – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-01

VibeCheck ("the Bot", "we", "us") is a community-analytics Discord bot operated by the Warpath & Palmon: Survival community operations team at Lilith Games. This policy explains what data the Bot handles, why, how long we keep it, and how you can contact us.

The Bot runs only in the official community Discord servers for Warpath and Palmon: Survival. It is an internal operations tool; it is not a public, general-purpose bot.

What we access

Using Discord's Message Content intent, VibeCheck reads messages posted in the public channels of the servers listed above, together with the minimal metadata needed to analyze them:

  • message text
  • channel name
  • timestamp
  • the author's Discord display name

We do not read direct messages, and we do not operate in servers other than the official community servers above.

Why we use it

Message content is used solely to generate aggregated community-sentiment reports, feedback summaries, and issue/alert detection for the community team. This helps us understand player feedback, spot problems (bugs, payment issues, dissatisfaction) early, and respond faster.

We do not:

  • sell or rent your data;
  • share it with third-party advertisers;
  • use it to train AI models;
  • build advertising or cross-service profiles of individual users.

Automated processing

To produce summaries, message text is processed by an automated large-language-model service under contract to Lilith Games. This processing is used only to generate the summary and is not used to train any model.

How long we keep it

  • Raw message content is processed transiently and is not permanently archived. Any short-term working cache that contains message excerpts is automatically deleted within 30 days.
  • After that period, we retain only aggregated summaries and statistics for the community team's reference.

Where it is stored and how it is protected

  • Aggregated summaries are stored in our Feishu (Lark) enterprise workspace, which encrypts data at rest.
  • Working data on the operations team's managed systems is stored on encrypted-at-rest disks.
  • Access is restricted to the community operations team.

Your choices and rights

  • Deletion / access requests: email warpathcommunity@lilith.com and we will action your request for data associated with your Discord account.
  • Stop all collection: a server administrator can remove the Bot from the server, or stop it from monitoring a channel, at any time.

Children

The Bot is not directed to children under the age required by Discord's Terms of Service, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
